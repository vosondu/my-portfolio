Overview

Leading the visual design for Nigeria's premier blockchain conference.

It feels somewhat surreal to have led the visual design for BWBCON 2026. Regardless of how much work I'd put in to have earned this opportunity, it's clear that an opportunity like this couldn't have found me without some measure of grace.

Working under a team led by Mr. Obinna — a man who prioritises excellence in everything he touches, and who operates with deep faith and resilience — the bar was always set high. That standard shaped every design decision made on this project.

Event collateral

Bold, energetic, unmistakably blockchain.

The visual language for BWBCON 2026 had to communicate the energy and forward momentum of the Web3 space, while retaining the credibility expected of a premier conference. Every asset — from the announcement flyer to the call for sponsors — was built to command attention and inspire trust simultaneously.

Social media

Keeping the community engaged across the campaign.

A sustained social media campaign ran throughout the event's buildup — seasonal posts, countdown graphics, and community announcements — all maintaining the visual coherence of the BWBCON brand while keeping the audience energised and informed.
